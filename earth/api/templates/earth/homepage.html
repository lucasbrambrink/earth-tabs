Earth tabs

This is a chrome extension to pull in landscape photography from a variety of different open-source photo collections in every new tab.

After installing the chrome extension, every new tab you open will show a photograph from the catalogue. Make sure you use the settings to customize your experience!

It was made with ♥ for outdoor enthusiasts and nature-lovers who enjoy wonderful landscape photography as part of their work day.

If you are curious, you can check out the source code. Feel free to email me with any questions or concerns that you might have!
