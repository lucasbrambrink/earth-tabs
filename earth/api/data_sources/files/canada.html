List of mountains of Canada

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search
Main article: Mountain peaks of Canada

The vast majority of mountains in Canada lie in the western provinces of British Columbia, Alberta, and the Yukon Territory. Mountains can be found all over British Columbia while those in Alberta are mainly situated on the eastern side of the Canadian Rockies. The Saint Elias Mountains in the Yukon hold some of country's highest mountains, including the highest, Mount Logan at 5,959 meters.
